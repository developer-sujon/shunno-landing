Informative Dashboard

Hishab Nikash provides inventory, stock and product Management as well as calculation of stock according to purchase price and selling price.It provides different filters.

Sell And Invoice

The software provides product sale through barcode scanning as well as quick sale, digital invoice and print facility. Daily, weekly, monthly and yearly transaction accounting invoice are also generated efficiently.

E-commerce store

The website of Hishab Nikash works exactly like an E-commerce Store with some exiting features.

Supplier And Customer Management

Ledger of supplier-retailer-customer has been generated. Facility to keep account of dues of customers and facility of sending SMS to customers in case of dues collection is provided.

SMS Portal

Hishab Nikash has installment payment alert SMS, Sales Payment Confirmation SMS, Dues Collection SMS is implemented along with some other exciting features.

Report

Daily purchase and sale report, profit-loss report, daily income and expenditure report, bank transaction report, facility to print reports of all accounts are some exciting features.

Staff Management

Facility to keep track of business expenses including staff salary. Admin, Manager and Salesman ID has individual importance.

Data Backup

Facility to keep track of business expenses including staff salary. Auto backup facility of all accounts and data. The facility of using the software on multiple mobiles and computers from anywhere.

EMI facilities

Facility of installment collection by Installment Collector. Facility of selling goods in installments which is an EMI selling system

Security

The security of the website is very good. Proper Authentication and authorization has been utilized for top security
